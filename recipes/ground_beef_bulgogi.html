Ground Beef Bulgogi (quick and painless!)

This ground beef bulgogi dish only takes 15 minutes to cook, is super budget friendly, and a perfect fallback for when you you just don't feel like it! This recipe only calls for a measly 11 ingredients total, but is so savory and delicious it'll fit right into your regular rotation!

Whether you're hankerin' for a hearty korean dish or just need to eat but don't feel like using your brain, you've found the perfect recipe. The Bold bulgogi flavors paired with the juicy beef make for a fantastic little trip for your taste buds! I highly recommend serving this dish over fluffy white rice or with a side of steamed veggies to make a complete meal!
