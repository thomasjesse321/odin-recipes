Perfect White Rice in 4 Steps

Description

If you often find yourself struggling to get that restaurant quality rice in your kitchen at home then you're in the right place. This recipe will get you that perfect, soft, fluffy rice we all love. Say hello to your new rice standard!

This is a simple 4 step recipe that takes little to no time. Most of the work is done by the pot and the stove, so you can easily set it and forget it if you're also in the process of cooking something else to go along with the rice. This recipe makes two serving of rice. To make a bigger batch of this recipe, simply add 2 cups of water for every one cup of rice. I love how easy and painless this recipe is and I think you will too.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup white rice
  • pot

Steps

  1. Boil Water: Get started by boiling 2 cups of water in an uncovered pot. When the water has reached a boil, add 1/4 teaspoon of salt and 1 cup of white rice.

    Pro Tip: To up the flavor, swap the 2 cups of water for 2 cups of broth (vegetable, chicken, or beef!)

  2. Let Simmer: Cover the pot and let it simmer for 18-20 minutes. As tempting as it may be, try not to stir or lift the lid while the rice is cooking.

    Pro Tip: When reheating, prevent cooked rice from drying out by breaking it up and adding a splash (or two) of water. Microwave on high heat until the rice is cooked through, about 1-1/2 minutes.

  3. Let Sit: Once the rice looks as fluffy as can be, take it off the burner and let it sit for 5 minutes. This cooling process allows the rice to steam and become the soft - but not too soft - consistency that we all strive for.

    Pro Tip: Don't ditch the leftovers. Store cooked rice in airtight containers for up to 6 days after cooking.

  4. Fluff and Finish: Add more texture to the rice by fluffing it with a fork. Before serving, top it off with 1/2 cup of chopped herbs (like cilantro or parsley!).
