Perfect White Rice in 4 Steps

Description

If you often find yourself struggling to get that restaurant quality rice in your kitchen at home then you're in the right place. This recipe will get you that perfect, soft, fluffy rice we all love. Say hello to your new rice standard!

This is a simple 4 step recipe that takes little to no time. Most of the work is done by the pot and the stove, so you can easily set it and forget it if you're also in the process of cooking something else to go along with the rice. I love how easy and painless this recipe is and I think you will too.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup white rice
  • pot
